Welcome to Hart County

Hart County is located in northeast Georgia and is known for its strong community, scenic beauty, and proximity to Lake Hartwell. This page provides a brief overview of the county’s history, geography, and civic structure. (Demo content for presentation.)

Established: 1853
County Seat: Hartwell
Region: Northeast Georgia

History

Hart County was formed in the mid-19th century and named in honor of Revolutionary War figure Nancy Hart. Over time, it developed as a rural agricultural community and later expanded with tourism and recreation centered around Lake Hartwell.

Geography & Recreation

The county features rolling hills, farmland, and access to Lake Hartwell — a major recreational destination for boating, fishing, and camping. Parks and outdoor amenities contribute to the area’s quality of life.

Government Structure

Hart County operates under a Board of Commissioners form of government. Elected officials oversee administration, budgeting, infrastructure, and public services.

Community & Services

The county provides essential services including public safety, road maintenance, parks, planning and zoning, elections administration, and tax services.
